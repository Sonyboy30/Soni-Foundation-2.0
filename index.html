About Us

Our mission is to make a direct impact on society, by supporting Research & Development in Health Care, aiding Family Health Support, and by disrupting the barriers to Education.

The Soni Family Foundation was founded by Arav Soni and his father, Manmeet Soni, with the vision of creating a lasting impact in the fields of Research & Development, Family Health Support, and Education. Arav Soni is a dedicated entrepreneur and philanthropist passionate about driving positive change. His father, Manmeet Soni, brings a wealth of experience and wisdom, ensuring the foundation remains true to its mission of breaking barriers and fostering innovation in healthcare and education.

Our Programs

Education Access Initiative: Providing scholarships and learning resources to underprivileged students.

Family Health Support: Assisting families with medical care, mental health services, and wellness programs.

Healthcare R&D Grants: Funding innovative research to advance medical breakthroughs.
